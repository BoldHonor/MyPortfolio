Procedural maze Generating Game

Procedural Generation has been the game changer ever since it was introduced
Many world wide hit games like 'MineCraft' use this technique to give a continious feel of new enviournment and ever changing levels. 2D games like 'Spelunky' (A dungoen crawler) have its effectiveness  and increased the factor of exploration . the random generation is partially controlled as to account for feasibility of games.
This Program here generates 2d mazes that can be made 3d using 'ray engines '
the maze is always solveable and has a limit to max corridors and rooms also only Horizontal or Vertical Movement is allowed because ray tracer engines generally  prefer 2 directional mazes.
